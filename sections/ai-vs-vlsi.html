⚔️ AI vs VLSI

The debate that never ends

A (VLSI):

Without hardware, nothing exists. Silicon is reality.

B (AI):

Without intelligence, hardware does nothing. Software runs the world.

A (VLSI):

Performance, power, reliability — all start at the transistor level.

B (AI):

Decision-making, automation, prediction — all start at the algorithm level.

Conclusion:

AI needs hardware to exist. Hardware needs intelligence to matter.

The future doesn’t belong to one field — it belongs to people who understand both :)
